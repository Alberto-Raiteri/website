Agnese's Book

1997

Dimensions: 32x25,5 cm
Ink, watercolor and acrylic on canvas

Agnese's Book is a book for children.

It includes fourteen unpublished and original fairy tales and six short interludes, written and illustrated by hand on canvas and subsequently bound in a single copy.

Dedicated to little Agnese, the book wants to be understood as an artist's book dedicated by a father to his newborn daughter.
